A book about Luis Barragán

Completed under Mark Laughlin at Northeastern University for Typography II.

Instructed to design a book that assembled text and image and utilized at least three types of textual information, I leapt at an opportunity to design a book about an architect. I selected Luis Barragán, a Mexican architect who inspired my grandfather – and referenced his use of geometry, color, simple forms, and movement.
