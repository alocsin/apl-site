Baugruppe translated from German to English literally means "building group" and is a model of housing that falls somewhere between communal housing and a condominium association. The end product depends on the group that comes together to build a baugruppe: financing, social make-up, quality of living, and ultimately, the resulting architectural and urban qualities of the structure, are all up to the residents.
